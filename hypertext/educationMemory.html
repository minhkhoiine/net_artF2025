My beloved schools

Dao Duy Anh, DDA, was my middle school. In Vietnam, middle school will take 4 years from grade 6 to 9. At DDA, I met lots of friends who colored my childhood not to be boring and forgettable. At DDA, I joined the mathematics team to compete in the city math olympiad. I was a top and famous alumni there.

Le Quy Don, LQD, was my high school. I stayed there for three years. I met my close friends there. We spent time studying, doing projects, and enjoying our youth. One thing that I regret the most of LQD is I did not make more friends. People there are amazing. I met my soulmate there. However, after I went to the US, we did not talk much. Now, we are only memory of each other. I pray her all the best. I am proud that now she could be independent without me. I'm so proud of you!!!
